How It works



1- Choose your product

Choose your product Select the brand you want to shop for. giftvipcard supports 100’s of gift cards and vouchers and top-ups in 180 brand. Make sure to add to cart the product available .

2- Select your currency and package

giftvipcard offers the most competitive prices for products you love and top ups for your mobile operator. Just select the cryptocurrency you wish to use to pay for your purchase and the package. When you buy on giftvipcard you will automatically earn giftvipcard points. As soon as you have enough points you can redeem your points for Crypto.

3- no need to create account

no need to create an account, you may just input the email you want to use in the transaction.

4- Pay with your crypto

Pay via option will allow you to switch to cryptocrrancy Pay if you want to directly pay from your crypto wallet. The default wallet option will let you choose between different crypto and networks. Please pay close attention to the network. To be able to use the network requires your wallet to support it. Sending stable coins on the wrong network will result in losing your funds. Please make sure you set the right fee for the transaction some wallets don’t show proper fees estimations. More information about transaction fee can be found in our FAQ fee section. Check our frequently asked questions about a payment on our FAQ page payment section.

5- Receive your product instantly

We immediately deliver your product as soon as your payment is confirmed on the blockchain. Please do not forget to check your spam folder in your email. Please take a look at our FAQ , where you can find answers to many questions about your transaction.
